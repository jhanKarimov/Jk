Images that already sounded like prose.

This page starts turning the photo archive into a literary one. Each pairing pulls from the shared gallery manifest and places it beside an English fragment from Between Being and Nothingness, building a more indexable and legacy-oriented version of the JK universe: part novel, part visual diary, part web archive.

Chapters Used The Roar of Silence, Euthanasia Camps, and Lara.
Photo Window Mostly 2020–2022 favorites and selected public archive work.
Intent Give the photos language, give the book a visible body, and let search engines index the archive as authored work.

Next evolutions for this mode: chapter-specific galleries, quote randomizer, bilingual toggles, city timelines, and single-photo permalink pages that join image, caption, quote, place, and year into one archival page.
